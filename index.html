What is a coffee cherry?

What is a coffee cherry? Quoting from Healthline, the coffee fruit which is also known as coffee berry and coffee cherry is a stone fruit from the coffee plant which is home to coffee beans. Coffee cherries are initially green which then turn purplish red as they ripen.
